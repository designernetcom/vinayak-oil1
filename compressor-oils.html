Compressor Oils

Compressor Oils are a vital component in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of compressors, which are widely used across various industries. SPEL offers high-quality compressor oils designed to meet the demanding requirements of different compressor types and operating conditions. Compressor used in high-speed centrifugal compressors, ensuring stability and minimizing the risk of foaming and cavitation.

Importance of Selecting the Right Compressor Oil

Choosing the correct type of compressor oil is critical to ensure the longevity and performance of the compressor. Factors such as the type of compressor, operating conditions, and the specific requirements of the application must be considered when selecting the appropriate oil. Regular maintenance and oil changes are also essential to maintain the efficiency and reliability of the compressor.

SPEL's range of compressor oils is designed to meet the diverse needs of these applications, ensuring optimal performance, protection, and longevity for various types of compressors in different industries.

Types of Compressor Oils
Mineral-Based Compressor Oils

These are formulated from high-quality base oils and are suitable for general-purpose use in compressors operating under moderate conditions.

Synthetic Compressor Oils

Made from advanced synthetic base stocks, these oils offer superior performance in extreme conditions, including high temperatures and pressures, and extended oil drain intervals.

Food-Grade Compressor Oils

These specialized oils are designed for use in food processing applications where incidental contact with food is possible. They comply with strict food safety regulations.
